1. What is CRM?

CRM (Customer Relationship Management) is a system that helps businesses manage customer data, interactions, and sales activities in one place.

2. Why CRM is Important

Without CRM, customer details can be forgotten. CRM ensures no customer or follow-up is missed.

3. CRM in Retail Stores

Retail stores handle many walk-in customers. CRM helps store customer interests and purchase intent.

4. Lead Management

Leads are customers interested in products. CRM stores leads with contact details and requirements.

5. Product Tracking

Products are stored in CRM so leads are always linked to available items.

6. Reminder System

CRM reminders help employees follow up with customers at the right time.

7. Customer Response Tracking

CRM records whether a customer responded, helping prioritize serious buyers.

8. Lead Scoring & Priority

Leads gain points based on responses. High-score leads get higher priority.

9. Deal Conversion

When a customer confirms purchase, the lead is closed and converted into a deal.

10. Why Our CRM is Simple

Our CRM is designed to be simple, fast, and user-friendly, so employees can focus on customers, not software.
