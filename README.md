# Customer Relationship Management (CRM) System

## Overview

The Customer Relationship Management (CRM) System is a web-based application designed to help businesses organize and manage their interactions with customers. The system provides a centralized platform where users can track potential customers (leads), manage available products, record successful sales (deals), and schedule reminders for follow-ups.

The primary objective of this project is to demonstrate how a CRM system can streamline customer management and improve the sales process by organizing information in a structured and efficient way.

---

## System Objectives

* To manage customer leads effectively
* To track products offered by the business
* To record successful sales transactions
* To manage follow-up reminders with customers
* To provide a clear overview of sales activities through a dashboard

---

## System Modules

### Dashboard

The dashboard acts as the central overview of the system. It provides summarized information about key activities such as the total number of leads, products, closed deals, and reminders. This allows users to quickly understand the current status of business operations.

### Lead Management

The lead management module is used to store and organize information about potential customers. Each lead represents a possible business opportunity. Managing leads helps businesses track prospects and focus on converting them into customers.

### Product Management

The product management module maintains a list of products offered by the business. It allows the organization to keep track of product information that can be presented to potential customers during the sales process.

### Deal Management

The deals module represents successful sales transactions.
When a lead decides to purchase a product, the lead is marked as closed and recorded as a deal.

This process follows a simple flow:

Lead → Customer purchases a product → Lead is closed → Recorded as a Deal

The system tracks these deals to monitor completed sales and overall business performance.

### Reminder Management

The reminder module helps users keep track of important follow-ups with customers. Reminders ensure that communication with potential or existing customers happens on time, helping maintain strong relationships and improving customer satisfaction.

---

## System Workflow

The CRM system follows a structured workflow:

1. Potential customers are added to the system as leads.
2. Products offered by the business are maintained within the system.
3. When a lead decides to purchase a product, the lead is marked as closed.
4. The closed lead is recorded as a deal, representing a successful sale.
5. Reminders can be created to track follow-ups or important tasks related to customers.
